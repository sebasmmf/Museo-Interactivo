Who is

Animal Planet

Animal Planet is an American subscription television channel launched in 1996 that is owned by Discovery Inc. as a result of a sale of shares of the American Animal Planet made by the BBC to Discovery Networks, internationally; the channel was originally launched as a joint venture between BBC Worldwide and Discovery Networks. The channel is dedicated to programming on the relationship between humans and animals, it is the sister channel of Discovery . It is available in more than 70 countries around the world and has local versions in countries such as Canada, the United Kingdom and India.
